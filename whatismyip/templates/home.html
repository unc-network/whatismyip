What does this tool show?

All visitors see:

  • IPv4 and IPv6 address detection
  • Internet Service Provider (ISP) and geolocation
  • Internet DNS provider and security filtering status
  • IP geolocation map (city-level accuracy)

Campus and VPN visitors also see:

  • Campus network and VLAN assignment
  • Network Access Control (NAC) details
  • Building location map (street-level, where available)

Knowing your network details helps diagnose connectivity problems before calling the help desk. VPN users can confirm their traffic is routing through campus before accessing IP-restricted resources such as licensed databases or administrative systems. Wireless users can verify they are on the correct network segment. If something looks wrong — an unexpected ISP, an off-campus address while on-site, or a missing VPN address — this page gives you the specific details to share with ITS support.
