About "What is my IP?"

This site is hosted on the campus network and provided by Information Technology Services as a network diagnostic tool. The IP addresses reported are those visible to campus applications such as addresses provided by the campus VPN service. Refer to the Determine Client IP Address help article for site questions or comments. Further detailed network information is available through the Network Information Tool (NIT).
